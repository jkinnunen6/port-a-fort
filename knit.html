      Privacy: This page never records, stores, or transmits audio — it has no server and nothing here is sent anywhere by this page. When listening is on, your browser's built-in speech service converts what it hears into text; depending on your browser, that conversion may happen on the browser maker's servers (Chrome, for example) or on your device (Safari, in most cases). This page only ever receives the finished text, checks it for command words, and immediately discards it. Your pattern, progress, and settings are saved solely in this browser's local storage on your own device.
