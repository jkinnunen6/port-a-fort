Project Integral Wellness Institute
Type Design / Development
Stack WordPress / PHP / CSS
Year 2026

A fully custom WordPress build for a two-founder wellness consultancy specialising in workplace resilience, executive coaching, and clinical health. Inheriting early design direction, I took the project through design refinement and built every template, component, and interaction from scratch in PHP and CSS — no page builders, no pre-built theme.

Live site — scroll within the frame to explore

01

The Brief

Integral Wellness Institute needed a digital presence that could carry the authority of two seasoned specialists — a master-certified executive coach and a licensed naturopathic doctor — while remaining approachable to the corporate clients and executives they serve. The site needed to communicate three distinct service areas clearly without fragmenting the brand.

02

The Build

Every page is a custom PHP template — no Elementor, no Divi, no shortcode soup. CSS was written by hand throughout, with a consistent design system governing spacing, type, and colour. The three service pillars — Workplace, Executive, and Clinical Wellness — each required their own content architecture while sharing a unified visual language.
