A fully custom WordPress build for a two-founder wellness consultancy specialising in workplace resilience, executive coaching, and clinical health. Inheriting early design direction, I took the project through design refinement and built every template, component, and interaction from scratch in PHP and CSS with no page builders or pre-built themes.

The Brief

Integral Wellness Institute needed a digital presence that could carry the authority of two specialists while remaining approachable to the corporate clients and executives they serve. The site needed to communicate three distinct service areas clearly without fragmenting the brand.

02

The Build

Built from scratch in PHP and CSS with AI assistance. A consistent design system governs spacing, type, and color across every template. The three service pillars, Workplace, Executive, and Clinical Wellness, each required their own content architecture while sharing a unified visual language. A custom contact form handles enquiries directly without third-party plugins, and a bespoke articles template lets the client publish new content independently, with every post automatically inheriting the site's full visual styling.
